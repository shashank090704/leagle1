Chapter 1: The Post Office Murder Case

Barendra Kumar Ghose was not directly involved in the murder or robbery but was charged under Section 34 for common intention.

What will happen next?

Relevant Laws:

  • Section 34 - Common Intention
  • Section 302 - Punishment for Murder
  • Section 394 - Voluntarily causing hurt in committing robbery

Even though Barendra Kumar Ghose didn't personally commit the murder or robbery, Section 34 of the IPC allows for prosecution under common intention. He was found guilty for both the murder and robbery under this section.
